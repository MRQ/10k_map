Getting to the CCCAC Hackerspace

Chaos Computer Club Aachen e.V.
Jülicher Straße 191
52070 Aachen
Germany

The entrance is on the right side of Krantzstraße (house number 10). That is a side street of Jülicher Straße between Zentis and Talbot.

By public transport: The bus stop Talbot is right in front of Krantzstraße. If you come from outside of Aachen, use the train station Aachen Hbf.

By individual transport: If you reach Aachen by Autobahn A 4 or A 44, switch to A 544 after the crossing Aachener Kreuz. Go from Europaplatz to Jülicherstraße by the Joseph-von-Görres-Straße.
